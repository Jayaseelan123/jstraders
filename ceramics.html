THE CERAMIC ART

Purity in Every Curve

Nano-Glaze Tech

Our ceramics are fired at 1280°C with an ultra-smooth Nano-glaze that prevents dirt from sticking, ensuring a permanent shine.

High Pressure Cast

Unlike standard ceramics, our pieces are high-pressure cast to eliminate internal air pockets, resulting in 30% higher impact resistance.

Stain Resistance

The non-porous surface is resistant to chemical agents and acidic cleaners, maintaining its pristine white finish for decades.

VITREOUS CHINA

The Secret to Longevity

JS Traders ceramics are crafted using premium Vitreous China, a material known for its incredible strength and low water absorption rate (below 0.5%). This makes our washbasins and WCs the most hygienic choice for your home.

Every piece undergoes a multi-stage inspection process to ensure there are no ripples, pinholes, or unevenness in the glaze, providing you with a product that is practically a work of art.

  • Material: Premium Vitreous China
  • Firing: 1280°C High-Temp Kiln
  • Absorption: Less than 0.5%
