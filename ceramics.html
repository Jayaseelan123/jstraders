Aura Vessel Basin

The Aura series is a testament to the beauty of thin-walled ceramic engineering. Featuring a sleek, geometric profile and a high-gloss finish that reflects the light beautifully.

Crafted from premium Vitreous China and fired at 1280°C, it provides a surface that is both incredibly hard and non-porous for ultimate hygiene.

Minimalism meets maximum hygiene. The Zen series features a rimless design that eliminates hidden crevices where bacteria can grow. The concealed mounting ensures a clean, uninterrupted look.

  • Flush: Rimless Dual-Cyclone
